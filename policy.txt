ACME Corp Customer Policy

Refund Policy:
At ACME Corp, we want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you may request a refund within 30 days of the purchase date.
- To request a refund, contact support@example.com with your order number and reason for the refund.
- Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after approval.
- Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return was due to our error.
- Items must be returned in original condition. Returned items that are damaged or altered may be subject to a partial refund or rejection.

Exchanges:
Exchanges for a different size or color are allowed within 30 days, subject to product availability. Contact support for assistance.

Exceptions:
- Custom or personalized items are final sale and not eligible for refunds unless defective.
- Digital goods (downloads, software licenses) are not refundable once delivered unless defective.

Contact:
For questions about refunds, returns, or exchanges, email support@example.com or call (555) 123-4567.
